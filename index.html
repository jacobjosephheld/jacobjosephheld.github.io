About

Jacob Joseph Held is a director working across documentary, narrative, and experimental film. His work is driven by an interest in the spaces between people — the silences, the glances, the things left unsaid.

With a background in photography and visual art, Jacob brings a painterly sensibility to the moving image. His films have screened at festivals worldwide, including Tribeca, SXSW, Sundance, and the Berlin International Film Festival.

He is currently based in New York and is in development on his debut feature film.
